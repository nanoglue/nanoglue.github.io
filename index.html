Our Mission

Every year, millions of patients worldwide receive skin transplants because of severe burns, sports injuries or diabetic wounds. One out of ten of these transplantations fails, often with dreadful complications. An aging society, the rise of diabetes and untreatable superbugs are bound to increase this failure rate. The last decades have brought little improvement in the field – most products focus on structure restoration or contain hugely expensive biologics. We believe it's time for a change. After decades of research, nanotechnology is mature enough to step into the breach and disrupt the wound care market

Our Technology

Our unique formulation based on cutting-edge nanotechnology boosts the body's own capacity of self-healing and keeps bacterial infections in check. Our nanoparticle paste helps surgeons who want to ensure patient safety and survival by reducing skin transplantation complications and provide swift healing.

  • Tissue bonding

    The so-called nano-bridging effect enables our nanoparticles to strongly connect different tissues. They ensure that transplants stay in place.

  • Regenerative

    Our unique formulation actively supports the body in healing itself. Improved regeneration means a higher chance of success and a faster recovery.

  • Antimicrobial

    Several antimicrobial materials within the nanoparticles keep infections in check. Only local application required and no danger of antibiotic resistances.
